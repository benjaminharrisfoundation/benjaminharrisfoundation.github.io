What you'll do

If you apply to volunteer you can expect to do activities such as:

  • Maintain this website
  • Help with the ben wall project
  • Help us branch into new areas
  • Community outreach, and support
  • Quality assurance
  • (and other items not listed here)

How to apply

To apply to join, please either: visit us in person, post your application on the ben wall, or email (at benjaminharrisfoundation@gmail.com) us with your contact information and how you can help.

More information

Benefits

If you become a volunteer, any donation will be split equally with you, and all other volunteers. Volunteering here means that you get to contribute positively to your community, in any way you can. If you volunteer here, you can tell all your friends that you work for a not–for–profit. Additionally, your peers will admire you for being part of a project that benefits society as a whole.
