What you'll do

If you apply to volunteer you can expect to do activities such as:

  • Maintaining this website
  • Help with the ben wall project
  • Helping us branch into new areas
  • Community outreach, and support
  • Quality insurance
  • (and other items not listed here)

How to apply

To apply to join, please either: visit us in person, post your application on the ben wall, or email us with your contact information and how you can help.

More information

Benefits

If you become a volunteer, any donation will be split equally with you, and all other volunteers. Volunteering here means that you get to contribute positively to your community, in any way you can. If you volunteer here, you can brag to all your friends that you work for a not–for–profit. Additionally, your peers will admire you for being apart of a project that benefits society as a whole.
