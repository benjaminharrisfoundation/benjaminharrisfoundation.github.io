The Benjamin Harris Foundation

Who are we?

The Benjamin Harris Foundation is a not for profit organisation dedicated to freedom, integrity, and whimsy.

What do we do?

The BHF tries to uphold it's principle ideals by: promoting integrity through honest communication, creating freedom via our public discussion forums, and inducing whimsy through what we do, and how we act.

The BHF also runs The Ben Wall (here). The Ben Wall helps honest and free discussion of our founder—Benjamin–and whimsy through how the project is facilitated.

How can you help?

To help you can either apply to be a member of the Benjamin Harris Foundation, or The Ben Wall
